Bird Neighbors

After discussing the scientific classification of birds by families,
Blanchan lists 19 "habitats" where birds can be found (such as "birds seen
near the edges of woods" and "birds found near salt water"),
and groups the birds themselves by size and color.The 52 color illustrations
were produced by photographing stuffed birds in front of appropriate backgrounds,
since cameras of the time could not take good photographs of living birds.
The published photographs were also limited by the color printing technologies of the time,
and some of the darker birds have been described as looking like
"they had been dipped in shoe polish
